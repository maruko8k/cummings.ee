e.e. cummings free poetry archive

Tulips and Chimneys

This 1923 release, published by Thomas Seltzer, was Cummings’s first book of verse. It entered the public domain in 2019.

The materials here come from a first-edition of the book scanned at the Los Angeles Public Library.

The table of contents is also available in json format.
