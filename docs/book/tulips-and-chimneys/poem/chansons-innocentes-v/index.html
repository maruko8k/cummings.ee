Chansons Innocentes V

Tumbling-hair
  picker of buttercups
  violets
dandelions
And the big bullying daisies
  through the field wonderful
with eyes a little sorry
Another comes
  also picking flowers

The poem was first published in “Tulips and Chimneys.” It is also available in YAML format.
